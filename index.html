My Creative Process

All of my projects are based off of a practiced formula to get the result that I am looking for

1

Discuss The Project

Bringing together all the relevant stakeholders to review the project's objectives, requirements, and constraints, and to come up with a plan for executing the project.

2

Brainstorming

Generate as many ideas as possible, without criticism or judgment, in order to find creative solutions to a problem.

3

UI/UX Planning

Creating a detailed plan for the user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design of a product or service and defining the goals and objectives of the design, identifying the target audience, and creating a strategy for delivering a high-quality user experience.

4

Interaction

Considering the context in which the user will be using the product or interface include factors such as the user's physical environment, their emotional state, or the device they are using to interact with the product.
